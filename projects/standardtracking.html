DESCRIPTION

Client reached out asking for a 3-ind-1 package flyer, that would be used to showcase his service, which is building websites for clients who wish to ship products and track their motion. Essentially, 3 flyers were needed to show;
- General information about the service
- Price list and packages offered
- Samples of work done

THOUGHT PROCESS

The first task for me was to know who the target audience is! Given that our client isn't looking to work only locally, a global audience was being reached at. Thus, getting clarity with the audience targeted made it sort of challenging, as we're looking at diversity in individuals across nations of the world.
In terms of age group, teens are less of the target and more of adults between ages 21 - 45.
This is what was concluded upon!

The complexity of the flyer was meant to be very very minimal, as the aim is for all information to be clearly seen throughout with little or no distractions on the design.
This brought in a rather subtle choice of colors; ones that are more easily visually comprehended by the brain in terms of familiarity, as well as fonts.

Flyer sizes were manipulated based on content per design but wasn't really a hassle, as it was mainly to be used for online publicity.

REQUIREMENT GATHERING

The client in this case was very generous to provide all information needed way before hand; such as the name, Standard Tracking, which was going to be the brand name. The contact information, and other discrete related content for the design as pleased.

From a graphic designer's point of view, it was worth noting FIRST that if there were going to be 3 designs for this same project, it's important to have a sense of familiarity between the designs.
Of course colors and fonts were played a strategic role in this, but a visual image would seal the deals. Hence, AY-DESIGNS sought out to creating a Logo-type representation primarily for use on the design and not as a commercial service provider.

FINISHING & REMARK

Overall, a time frame of 5 hours was used to complete the 3 designs needed, of course with back-to-back verification and corrections from the client. It was a task worth doing and has paid off for the client in his line of work.

Our Client Briefly Said; "Your designs are good. I had a significant increase in clients thanks to you. Love to continue working with you"
