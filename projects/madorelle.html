DESCRIPTION

Madorelle is a beauty care brand that specializes in all things cosmetics, that primarily look to enhace skin care as needed. Located in the environs of AFRICA, Madorelle is looking to going global. One method of this was company branding, of which the client in question sought to requesting for AY-DESIGNS' services to assist in product branding.

THOUGHT PROCESS

Skin care is not a thing of "age-group", and even when it is, we can take ranges from an average 16 year old teen to an average 60 year old. For this project, the target audience is of course the general public, irrespective of age, BUT looking critically at the much older set of people with some adequate understanding of WHAT exactly skin care products are for you.

Another point of focus here is gender! Although skin care is NOT limited to a certain gender, it is seen (especially in the given location of the company), that a huge percentage of the population attracted to this kind of service are the females.
With this in mind the focus primarily has to be directed towards them.

Skin Care product branding with flyer-type designs requires that the user's attention should be gotten at first glance. AY-DESIGNS aimed at targeting precisely, using images and colorful fonts, the user attention per type of product.

REQUIREMENT GATHERING

Due to the fact that there were multiple products being developed by Madorelle, multiple meetings were set-up where the names and description of each product were given. This of course helped AY-DESINGS know exactly how to convey the information needed.

The designs needed were all to be printed out at stickers to fit on the necessary products. Thus for each bottled product, precise dimensions were to be given such that there were no errors made.
Color choices were very STRICT as the client inteded that the products' color should match somehow with the sticker color. The color of products were given per product where possible and the design complemented it.
