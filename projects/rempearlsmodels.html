DESCRIPTION

Rem Pearls stands as a modelling group located in the Cameroon nation of Africa. Having numerous programs and events, AY-DESIGNS has been a trusted graphic designer working with them.

THOUGHT PROCESS

For each design, an audience of adults is key. Despite the age group, having professional looking designs are a major publicity step.
Each design is done with the thought process of the environment, categories of persons expected to be there, as well as theme of the event.

LUXURY, a keyword kept at the front of the head throughout workdone with Rem Pearls, has been solely used one way or the other whilst designing.

REQUIREMENT GATHERING

Due to the establishment with the client, there has been a solid understanding of how they work, and thus using strategies from previous work done is replicated one way or another but yet achieving diverse excellent results.

FINISHING & REMARK

In a nutshell, one has to undertand the aspect of showbiz whilst working for such clients. Whilst aiming at the general public to be part of the business (as a model), focus is also driven towards sponsors, other related brands that could be co-workers, etc.
This experience was one worthwhile and an eye opener to the world of designing in social arts.

Our Client Briefly Said; "AY-DESIGNS has been undoubtedly the best designer I've worked with in my show biz life. Success of a show could be determined by the quality and professionalism of flyers. AY-DESIGNS has been a key factor in my walk so far."
