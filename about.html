A DESIGNER BY GRACE

The year 2019 is when I got introduced to the art of graphic designing. It started off with the basic intention of wanting to eliminate a subject form it's background. Of course I wasn't looking for a high-tech software, so I resorted to a free online tool. My mentor however told me "it's not an efficient way to work", and further introduced me to photoshop.

I did all that was intended (to eliminate a background from an image) and I had no more work on there.
However, months passed with me wondering "what more could be achieved with the software?". So I ended up doing lazy learning

Push forward a couple of months, there was the need for workforce in graphic design at the Chuch I attend, and I knew just then, that the software wasn't made known to me by error. I took up the zeel to study for the core purpose of rendering a service to God.
From there, it's been an endless flow of excellence, as I always say;
